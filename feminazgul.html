Feminazgul Hard Enamel Pin

It started as a private joke, but here it is, in all its glory... my first pin design, the Feminazgul pin !

Made of hard enamel, it has silver plating and comes with two black rubber backings! Size is about 1,5 inches. There's also two little spots of black glitter, for that finish touch!

About Grades :
Since some of the pins can come with minor to eye-catching little flaws, I have thoroughly sorted them into two grades of quality. The A grade is what I expect the pin to look like, and the B grade contains pins with some small to noticeable flaws. But I hope you'll give those peculiar babies some love, too !

About Shipping :
I try to ship the pins on the next (working) day after your order, and I actually can offer shipping with tracking! But please note that for some countries, the tracking might be stopped at France's borders.
